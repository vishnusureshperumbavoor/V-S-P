Christmas Battle

OpenAI has completed their 12 days of shipmas. They have announced/released a bunch of stuffs. To counter it or something, Google has also announced a bunch of products in the December itself. Let's start with what OpenAI has released as a part of their 12 days of shipmas.

Day 1: $200/month ChatGPT Pro plan

Unlimited access to OpenAI o1, o1-mini, GPT-4o, Advanced voice

Day 2: Reinforcement Fine-Tuning

This fine tuning technique enable ML engineers to create expert models fine-tuned to excel at specific sets of complex, domain-specific tasks such as law, insurance, finance, healthcare etc.

Day 3: Sora

OpenAI's video generation models can be accessed through the $20 (plus) and $200 (pro) monthly plans.

Day 4: Canvas

An interface for writing and coding in collaboration with ChatGPT. Mini code editor.

Day 5: Apple Intelligence

ChatGPT will be available in Apple Intelligence.

Day 6: Advanced voice with video & Santa mode

Bring video to voice mode.

Day 7: Projects in ChatGPT

Day 8: Search

Day 9: APIs for developers

Day 10: ChatGPT on whatsapp (ww) and Phone calls (US)

Day 11: Work with apps
