World in 2025

The humans were enjoying ruling the earth for 300,000 years. But for the first time since the anthropocene epoch, humans started worrying about the appraisal of a new species. A species which might become far more intelligent than humans - Artificial Intelligence.
Even though it was existed for more than 60 years back the worry started since the release of chatGPT in 2022 December. The world was changed for ever.

Text Generation Models

Proprietary models

ChatGPT started the race to AGI journey with GPT-3. After that Anthropic joined the race. Now google and Chinese model trying to catch up on these space. There are only 2 major players in the proprietary market. GPT-4 (OpenAI) and Claude (Anthropic). Gemini (Google) needs to catch up.

Open Source models

Falcon from UAE then Mistral from France started the trend of open sourcing the model. Then both are absolutely mogged by Llama-3 (Meta). Now Zuck announced Llama-4 in 2025. But the thing is open source models stated getting competition from China also. Deepseek from China has a huge context length. Alibaba's Qwen is also catching up. Personally I have used Qwen for medical imaging capabilities and it is really good.

Image Generation Models

Even though DallE was existed before the arrival of ChatGPT people started wondering at the generated images only after the arrival of MidJourney. But the wow factor started shading after the arrival of the video gen models. Now almost all major LLMs are multimodals and are capable to generate good images. Because of most of them are fine tuned on open sourced stable diffusion or flux.

Video Generation Models

Video gen models will be the heated topic in AI in 2025. People wondered after seeing the demo of Sora in 2024 February. But as of 2024 December only demo is there nothing is released from the side of OpenAI. We have witnessed lot of good videos generated by Kling (China), Runway ML, Pika Labs, Luma (US). Impressive part is the open sourced ones Mochi1 (US) and Hunyuan (China) are on par with the closed ones. Pepsi has released an ad fully generated by AI. I have good 30 seconds animated short films. I'm expecting to see a good realistic reels/short film in 2025 and a fully AI generated movie in 2026. 2025 will be the year of video gen models.
