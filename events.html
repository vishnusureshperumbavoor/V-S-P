Vaiga Agri Hack 2023

Event : Hackathon
Organized by : Kerala Government + Department of Agriculture Development and Farmers' Welfare
Date : 25/02/2023 - 27/02/2023
Venue : College of Agriculture, Vellayani, Thiruvananthapuram
Result : Won 3rd prize

We won 3rd prize in this 36 hour event and was able to talk with h'ble agricultural minister of Kerala. VAIGA Agri Hack 23 is Kerala’s largest agricultural hackathon conducted as a part of the VAIGA Agricultural Fest, participating college students, startups, individuals, farmers, innovationists, and the public.

Me & Afwan Sha came back from coimbatore paper presentation event & joined with our team. There were 5 members in our team including us and Rhithuja, Akhiyar, Shinas . They were already there on 24th. My price prediction were rejected by them one week back & now I joined Brown Plant Hopper detection problem statement.

It was a 36 hour hackathon & the participants were not allowed to sleep between. Already it was sleepless nights for me last 2 days. Our project was BPH detection using Low Powered Wide Area Network. Our product wasn't organized that much in the beginning. But it started to improve after each jury visit. I built 2 ML models based on eXtreme Gradient Boosting. After 36 hours both teams from our college selected to the final round. After the presentation round, the winners were announced & we were third. 3rd in a state level hack is not bad. Right??

We received the price on March 2nd at Putharikandam Maidanam, Thiruvananthapuram. We are also planning on upgrading the project into a startup.

Rajasthan IT Hackathon - 2k23

Event : Hackathon
Organized by : Rajasthan Government + Information Technology and Communications, Rajasthan
Date : 19/03/2023 - 21/03/2023
Venue : University Rajasthan College

Organized by the Department of IT&C, Rajasthan Hackathon 7.0 is a 24 hour non-stop coding marathon. The program started at 19/02/2023 10.00 PM & ended at 21/03/2023 10.00 AM. It was stupendous experience for me to participate in a national level cosmopolitan event. There were 3 members in my team including me, Afwan and Maheshwaran Mayavan from TN. It was first time I'm working with a member outside of the state.

We were planning to do a tourist automation project based on smart city and infrastruture. But we didn't know they will give the problem statement only at 10.00 PM. There were total 12 problem statements. And we had doubt which problem to choose. I wanted to try supply chain management using blockchain. Maheshwaran wanted to try financial fraud detection. But to make it interesting we decided to do locust attack detection using LoRa sensor. But what lacked was the hardware. We decided to give it a shot on the software part. We collected details related to the ideal condition for the existence of locust & created ML model on eXtreme Gradient Boosting. It was all perfectly fine. But what I understood from this hackathon was the IOT devices are the heart of every hackathons.

Rajasthan cold temperature was one hell of an experience for me. I thought rajasthan is a hot city before coming to this event. It was cold there even during afternoons. Made lot of friends from multiple cities like Pune, Chennai, Noida, Ahmedabad, Lucknow etc. The main agenda for participating in this event was to expand the network all over India.

Awega - 2k23

Event : Startup Idea presenation
Organized by : MES AIMAT
Date : 13/02/2023
Venue : MES AIMAT Marampilly
Result : Won 2nd prize

We won 2nd prize in this event. But the special thing was this event was the beginning of everything. This was the foundation for Thiruvananthapuram, Rajasthan, Coimbatore, Palakkad and many more to come events.

SanTekFest - 2k23

Organized by : Sankara College, Coimbatore
Date : 24/02/2023
Venue : Sankara, College Coimbatore
